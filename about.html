About Us

History

Tekkie Car Wash is a company dedicated to transforming dirty and greasy cars to clean, fragrant, spotless and shiny
vehicles that impress and look appealing to the eye. This company was firstly established by Nathi Ngomane in the
year 2010. It started out as a small car wash but but ended up having a second branch at Ivory Park in 2017. By the
year 2022 both branches were renovated and thats when Tekkie Car Wash became famous in the neighborhood

Our Vision & Mission

Our mission is to provide professional and the highest quality of service to all our customers. It is also our priority
to leave our customers satisfied and withot any complaints. It is also our number 1 goal to make every car clean, dirtless
and fragrant.
