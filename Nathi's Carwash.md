Website Proposal for Tekki Car Wash

#1. Organization Overview:
Name: Tekki Car Wash 

#Provide a brief history of the organization.
Tekki Car Wash was first established by Nathi Ngomane in 2010 at Winnie Mandela Park. As it started out as a small car wash, it developed a second branch in Ivory Park in 2017 and renovated the main branch.

#Mission and vision statements.
Our mission is to provide professional and the highest quality of service to all our customers. Also, we make it our priority to leave every customer satisfied and with no complaints. It is also our priority that we leave each and every car clean and spotless. Our vision is seeing ourselves as one of the most recognized Car Washes in the nation and serving clients of high stature and dignity
#Target audience.
	Those who want a clean, spotless, fragrant, scented, and appealing car.
	People who do not have time to wash their cars but want them to be always clean.
	Those who want to witness and see the skills and performance that can be provided by Tekki Car Wash.
#2. Website Goals and Objectives:
To practice car washing that is friendly to our staff members, community and especially the environment. Providing professional and quality services to our customers with the lowest prices. To become the most trusted name when it comes to car hygiene reliance and top-notch services (Kingsley, 2025).
It is also the car wash’s objective to have an increasing number of visitors and online enquires. This will enhance the dignity and popularity of the website, which also improves the production and profit of the car. There will be a comment section on our website so that we can hear from our customers. This will enable them to tell us where we need to improve our services.
#3. Current Website Analysis
The strengths of the website will be that the website is centered among customers, meaning it will be designed for the purpose of customers communicating with the car wash. They will find useful and relevant information with an easy-to-use interface. Some of the weaknesses can be that the website may contain a lot of information that requires a lot of reading from the customer. There can also be a lack of mobile support and access to the website (Kucheriavy, 2016).
The car wash plans to have advertisements on TV and the Radio for those who do not have mobile devices. There will also be summarized information for less reading. These are there to improve the website’s weaknesses.

#4. Proposed Website Features and Functionality
The website will be four pages, namely:
•	Home Page
•	Services 
•	Contact us 
•	About us
•	Enquiries 
The Home page is the introduction to our website; it is the face of our company. From the Home page you can navigate via link to the Services page where you will have access to all our services, from interior management to exterior management. Then the Contact us page has all our contacts including numbers you can call, and our email address and the About Us page will provide general information and extras. Then lastly the Enquiries page has a platform where our customers can be able to place any kind of questions they would like to ask. This page will also be helpful when it comes to the feedback we require as a company.

#5. Design and User Experience
Color Scheme
The website consists of a simple color palette that blends in with the business’s branding and supports information hierarchy. This makes it to be completely functional, both actively and efficiently across multiple devices (Csontos, 2025).
#Typography
There are a variety of fonts across the website providing a layout readable to the customer, but it is nothing too complex and will not pose a challenge to anyone who wants to communicate with the car wash. This enables the user to properly navigate through the website.
#Layout and Design
The Car wash’s website is going to have a Single Column Layout. This is because it continues to apply the same design throughout all devices. It also guides the user towards the content removing major distractions (Boag, 2026).

#6. Technical Requirements
The files that contain everything about the website are found in the Main folder which has all the pages and a sub folder where all the images are kept. All text files are done via HTML, and the styling is kept a minimum, but CSS was used on that, and JavaScript is not yet implemented.

#7. Timeline and Milestones
The website for Tekkie carwash should be up and running before the 20th of April 2026. The main objective is to have a website that works properly with about 4 pages that can navigate through. A small business website such as Tekkie Car Wash can take from 4 to 6 weeks to make, which is enough time given before the submission dates (Mika, 2026).
